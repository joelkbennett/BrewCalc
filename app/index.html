BrewCalc is a simple app to help speed up my volume calculations when brewing. I use a three kettle system, with a hot liquor, mash and boil kettle. By inputting the weight of grain used a recipe, this calculator will show the amount of water (in centimetres) required.

Checkout the project on GitHub.
